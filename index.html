Venerons Laboratories

Perpetual Testing Project

The Venerons Laboratories are laboratories created by Daniele Veneroni for creative projects of various kinds, ranging from software development, photography, design, music and sound production, the production of short films and more.

Often projects are initiated and then maintained or developed by the community and not just by the laboratories themselves. The purpose of Venerons Laboratories is to provide tools to the community or to create environments that encourage creativity and free thought.
