Why donate to the Venerons Laboratories?

The Venerons Laboratories aims to provide free and open tools to the community, and to provide good environments that encourage creativity and open thougth.

By the way, all this work have their cost, and takes a lot of free time hours.

Donations help to support costs and are a mental reenforcement to me that the work I do matters. More donations I receive, the more I'm motivated to spend my free time working on Venerons Laboratories projects.
